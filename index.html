Our manifesto

We are students, not engineers. We don't have degrees or years of experience. What we have is hunger — to understand how the internet works, to build things that matter, to break things ethically, and to grow into the developers and defenders this world needs. We are from Morocco. We learn in French. We code in English. And we dream without borders.

— The Syole Collective, 2024
